G.S. SAHOTA is Assistant Professor in the Department of Literature at the University of California at Santa Cruz and over the 2010-2011 academic year will be a research fellow in the "Europe in the Middle East / The Middle East in Europe" program at the Wissenschaftskolleg zu Berlin. His first book, Late Colonial Sublime: Neo-Epics and the Romantic Imagination in India, is due out in 2012 from the University of California Press (Flash Point Series). His interests revolve around questions of spirit and medium in Indian traditions, dialectics and postcolonialism, world literature, and cultural studies. He regularly translates literary works from the Urdu.
