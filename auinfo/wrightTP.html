THEODORE P. WRIGHT, JR., Professor Emeritus of the State University of New York at Albany, has done research on the politics of Indian Muslims and Muhajirin in Hyderabad, Bombay, Karachi and Lahore for forty years. He has published fifty-five articles and chapters in colloquia on various aspects of this subject. He studied beginning Hindustani at the University of California in the summer of 1967.
