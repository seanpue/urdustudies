SYED SARWAR HUSSAIN has taught English at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, and is now teaching at King Saud University. He is the author of Ideology and the Poetry of Stephen Spender (1998) and translator of Despairing Voices (2011), a volume of Urdu stories from India and Pakistan, and Ashes in the Fire (2012), a selection of Abdus Samad's short fiction works.
