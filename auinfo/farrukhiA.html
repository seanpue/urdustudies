ASIF FARRUKHI is a fiction-writer, critic and translator. He was born in 1959 in Karachi, studied medicine there and later earned a degree in Public Health at Harvard University. He currently works as a public health physician in Karachi. His latest publications include a volume of literary criticism Alam-e Ijaad
