RAZA ALI HASAN has published a book of poems, Grieving Shias (Sheep Meadow Press, 2006). He received an M.F.A. from Syracuse University where he was a University Fellow. He also holds an M.A. in English from the University of Texas at Austin. His poems have appeared in Agni, Tampa Review, Cimarron Review, Puerto del Sol and Poetry International. He is currently visiting professor at Iowa State University.
